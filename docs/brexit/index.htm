First time in forever, all UK AEGEE locals are working together to invite you to a thematic conference on Brexit.

The conference Brexit â What Next? will focus on the future of freedom of movement in the UK and its consequences for young people on both sides of the Channel. This advantage, which many of us take for granted, can be drastically limited for British people due to Brexit.

In which way will freedom of movement be limited? How will youth mobility be affected? Will Erasmus+ project still exist in the UK? To find out answers to these questions, and much more, apply for our event!

Apply

Apply now and enjoy our event with 25 partipants from all around Europe plus organisers in the beautiful cities of Sheffield and Manchester.

The only requirement prior applying to the event is that you must be member of some AEGEE antenna. If you're not yet a member you can become now at AEGEE-Manchester, AEGEE-Sheffield or AEGEE-London (follow these instructions).

Click and fill the below forms. You have until Tuesday 10th October to apply:

  1. The form on the AEGEE intranet.
  2. The event's Google Form.

Don't forget to fill in the Google Form after you applied on the AEGEE intranet. Both are obligatory parts of the application.

After the deadline you'll receive an email confirming whether you've been selected or not, and in case you've been, the instructions on how to pay the event fee.
